about me

Yooshin Kim

Hi, I'm Yooshin Kim, a M.S.&Ph.D. integrated course student at the department of Electrical Engineering & Computer Sciences (EECS) at DGIST, South Korea. I'm conducting my research under the supervision of Prof.Donghoon Shin at the DGIST Computational Theory and Application Lab.

My research spans anomaly detection and intrusion detection systems within industrial control systems, with a particular focus on ensuring the resilience and security of critical infrastructure. I am currently developing new approaches for user authentication that operate passively—without requiring additional user actions—while also advancing methods to detect and explain anomalous behaviors that could compromise large-scale industrial operations.
